Welcome to DivineGames.

A cleaner way to launch CrazyGames by slug.

Use the Bookmarklet

Drag this button to your bookmarks bar, then click it on a CrazyGames game page.

>Play DivineGames

Then go to CrazyGames and click the bookmarklet on a game.

Or Enter a Game Slug Manually

Copy the slug from the URL (example: crazy-flips-3d) and paste it below.

Tip: Press / to focus the slug input (your existing shortcut).
