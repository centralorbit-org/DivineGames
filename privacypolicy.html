Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 11, 2026

1) What this policy covers

This page explains what information DivineGames may process when you visit the site and how that information is used.

2) Information you provide

DivineGames does not require account creation. If you contact the site owner (for example, by email or through a form you add later), you may provide personal information such as your email address and the contents of your message.

3) Game slugs and usage

If you enter a game slug or open a URL like ?game=some-slug, that slug is used to load the requested game. The slug may appear in your browser history and in server logs (if your hosting provider logs requests).

4) Analytics (if enabled)

If you have analytics enabled (for example, PostHog), the analytics provider may collect device/browser information and event data (like which page you visited). If you don’t want analytics, remove the analytics script from your pages.

5) Cookies and local storage

DivineGames may use cookies or local storage for basic functionality (for example, saving preferences). Third-party services you embed may also set cookies.

6) Third-party content

Games and related assets are served by third-party providers. Those providers may collect information according to their own privacy policies. DivineGames is not responsible for third-party practices.

7) Security

No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. You use the site at your own risk.

8) Changes

This policy may be updated over time. Changes will be posted on this page with a new “Last updated” date.
