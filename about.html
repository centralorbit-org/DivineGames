About DivineGames

DivineGames is a fast, minimal UI for launching CrazyGames titles using a URL slug (for example: moto-x3m).

What it does

You can launch games by: using ?game=slug, entering a slug manually, or using the bookmarklet on CrazyGames.

What it is not

DivineGames is not affiliated with CrazyGames. Games and assets are owned by their respective creators and publishers.
